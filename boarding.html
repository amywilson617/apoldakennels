Home Away From Home

Apolda Kennels ensures that your dog has as many comforts of home as possible. We are pet owners at Apolda as well and deeply understand the role that pets play in our lives.
We are aware that you have put your trust in us and we will not let you down. When staying with us, your pets become our pets.

Boarding Information

If you would like to further simulate a home environment for your dog, you can bring their own food, however pet food is included in our boarding services. Please ensure that all food is in a sealed hard bottom container.
We welcome your pet’s toys, bedding, special treats, or anything that will make them feel more comfortable and at ease. Apolda will administer medications as needed, however an additional charge may apply.
We offer an individual area where your dog can enjoy the outdoors and can also place them into the community outdoor area if requested.

When planning your next out-of-town trip, please consider placing your trust in Apolda Kennels. We won’t let you down!
