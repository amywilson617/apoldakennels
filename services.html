Boarding

Pets don’t relish being away from their homes and apart from the people who love them. Apolda Kennels makes their experience fun, comfortable, and as close to home as possible. Let us host your pal!

Grooming

It is important to the health and hygiene of an animal to be well groomed. Our grooming services include bathing, brush-out, nail trimming, ear cleaning, and anal expression. We also offer haircuts.

Training

Apolda Kennels believes there is no such thing as a bad dog. A canine companions’ behavior can be altered with training sessions. We have the skills and experience to help mold your dog.
